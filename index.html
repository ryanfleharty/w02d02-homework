Instructions

Pokémon Battle Card Game is a game in which the player and computer opponent are initially dealt three random Pokémon character cards each. The computer's cards are laid face down. After the player selects a card for battle, the computer flips a card and the damage values are compared. If the player's chosen card has higher damage than the computer's, you win a point, or vice versa. Whomever has the most points from the individual matches will win the overall round. After three rounds, the participant who won the most rounds wins the game!

Good Luck!
